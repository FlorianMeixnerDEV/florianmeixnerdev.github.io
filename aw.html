Alan Walker Music - Wiki

Introduction to the Mod

The Alan Walker Music Mod is a mod about the DJ and songwriter Alan Walker.

The core of the mod is a custom music disc featuring some of his most famous songs.

Additional features and mobs are described below.

1. Custom Music-Disc

This mod includes special music discs with Alan Walker tracks:

  • Avalon
  • Believers
  • Creator Circle
  • Dust
  • Faded
  • Incommunicado
  • Moonshine
  • Old Habits
  • Time
  • Welcome To Walkerworld
  • Rise of the Drones
  • Sailing
  • Welcome to Lonely Club
  • ...and more

2. Custom Mobs

  • Alan Walker: Peaceful mob. Can be tamed with a diamond.
  • Bad Alan: Aggressive mob with red eyes.
  • The Guard: Protects you from evil mobs.

3. Installation

  1. Ensure Minecraft & NeoForge 1.21.8 are installed (link).
  2. Download the Alan Walker Music Mod .jar file.
  3. Place it in your Minecraft "mods" folder.
  4. Launch Minecraft with NeoForge to load the mod.

Via Modrinth

You can also install it using the Modrinth Launcher. Search for "Alan Walker Music" and follow prompts.

4. Commands

  • /summon alan_walker_music:alan_walker - Summons Alan Walker mob.
  • /summon alan_walker_music:bad_alan - Summons Bad Alan mob.
  • /summon alan_walker_music:the_guard - Summons The Guard mob.
  • /info - Display website info.
